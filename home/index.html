About One Innovation Hub Offa

ONE is an innovation hub for technology and entrepreneur training programs. Founded in year 2021, ONE innovation hub is a center that provides critical skills training, funding, and support in ICT and business ventures to entrepreneurs. Join us in providing opportunities to the next generation of world-class entrepreneurs from Offa and its Environs. ONE is an innovation hub located within the multi-sectorial Industrial Park in development in Offa. On completion, the Park shall be equipped with five (5) production zones to cater to entrepreneurs who would be engaged in the processing of agricultural products, solid minerals, production of polymer-based products, and creative industry ventures.
